Squeezeback Ad Format: TV Screen States

Video Content
State 1: Normal Playback
Content plays at full screen.
No ad displayed.
AD CONTENT
Video Content
State 2: Squeezeback Active
Ad expands from top (typically 40% of screen).
Content shrinks but remains visible.
Duration: 10-30 seconds typical.
Video Content
State 3: Return to Full Screen
Ad collapses.
Content returns to full screen.
Playback continues.

Technical Requirements

  • Player must support dynamic layout resizing
  • OpenRTB bid request must signal squeezeback capability
  • VAST creative must specify expansion dimensions
  • Typical expansion: 30-40% of screen height
  • Common use cases: Pause ads, mid-roll breaks, live event overlays
  • Content audio typically continues during squeezeback
